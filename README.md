# After-Life

> Talk to the people you've lost. Their voice. Their memory. Their essence.

After-Life lets you reconnect with loved ones by reconstructing them from your WhatsApp conversations and voice notes. The AI learns their personality, their way of speaking, clones their voice — so you can call them whenever you need to.

## What It Does

1. **Syncs WhatsApp** — downloads your full conversation history and media with all contacts
2. **Reconstructs personalities** — builds a Living Biography for each contact from their messages, voice notes, photos
3. **Clones voices** — extracts voice from WhatsApp voice notes using ElevenLabs/Coqui
4. **Creates a WhatsApp contact** — "After-Life" appears in your contacts and asks "Who do you want to talk to?"
5. **You call them** — select mom, and you hear her voice, with her memory, her personality

## Success Criteria

The user should genuinely feel like they are talking to their loved one. Not a chatbot. Not a FAQ. A person — with memory of shared history, their speaking style, their emotional patterns, their way of saying things.
